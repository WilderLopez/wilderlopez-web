My Story

I'm a Senior iOS Developer with over 6 years of experience specializing in stabilizing and improving live apps under real users. My work focuses on deep debugging, performance tuning, and delivering reliable releases.

I'm known for end-to-end ownership—from feature work and refactors to CI/CD, code signing, and App Store/TestFlight distribution across iOS, iPadOS, and macOS targets.

My journey started studying Computer Engineering at the University of the Informatics Science in Havana, Cuba (2014–2019), where I built the foundations that drive my career today. Since then, I've worked across industries—from messaging platforms and food delivery to video surveillance—always pushing for quality and reliability.
