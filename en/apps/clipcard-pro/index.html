ClipCard Pro

Your personal vault for sensitive data

iOS 16+ Public Beta
Try on TestFlight

ClipCard Pro is an iOS app designed to securely store and manage sensitive information like credit card numbers, crypto wallets, phone numbers, URLs, and free text.

Features

  • OCR Card Scanning - Capture credit card numbers using your camera
  • iCloud Sync - Your data synced automatically across all Apple devices
  • Biometric Auth - Protected with Face ID / Touch ID
  • Multiple Data Types - Credit cards, crypto wallets, phone numbers, URLs, free text
  • One-Tap Copy - Quick clipboard access
  • Drag & Drop - Organize your clips by dragging

Available in public beta. Try the app and share your feedback.
