ClipCard Pro

Your personal vault for sensitive data

iOS 17+ Public Beta
Try on TestFlight

ClipCard Pro is a secure iOS vault for your most sensitive data. Store credit cards, crypto wallets, tokens, NFTs, phone numbers, URLs, and free text — all protected with biometric authentication and synced across your devices via iCloud.

Features

  • 7 Data Types - Credit cards, crypto wallets, tokens, NFTs, phone numbers, URLs, and free text
  • OCR Card Scanning - Capture credit card numbers instantly using your camera with the Vision framework
  • iCloud Sync - Automatic synchronization across all your Apple devices
  • Face ID / Touch ID - Biometric protection on every app launch
  • Smart Clipboard Detection - Automatically detects and identifies data types from your clipboard
  • Siri Integration - Copy, search, create, and manage clips with voice commands
  • Privacy Masking - Granular control to hide sensitive data by type (last 4 digits, partial display)
  • 6+ Visual Themes - Customize the look with multiple themes, all with light and dark mode
  • Contact Association - Organize clips by linking them to your iPhone contacts
  • Import / Export - Backup and restore your data in JSON format
  • Gestures - One-tap copy, double-tap quick copy, swipe-to-delete, drag-and-drop reorder
  • Full Accessibility - VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion, and privacy-aware screen reading

Available in public beta. Try the app and share your feedback.
