Privacy Policy

ClipCard Pro

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ClipCard Pro ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our clipboard management application.

Data Storage

ClipCard Pro is designed with privacy as a priority. All clipboard data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your clipboard contents on external servers.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected

  • Basic device information for app functionality
  • Crash reports (optional, anonymized)
  • Usage analytics (optional, anonymized)

What We Don't Collect

  • Your clipboard contents
  • Personal identification information
  • Passwords or sensitive data you copy

Data Security

Your clipboard data never leaves your device. We use iOS security best practices to ensure your data remains protected and private.

Third-Party Services

ClipCard Pro may use analytics services to understand app usage patterns. These services receive only anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify individual users.

Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally, you have full control over it. You can:

  • Delete any or all clipboard history at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Opt out of analytics in the app settings

Contact Us
