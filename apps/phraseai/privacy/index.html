Privacy Policy

PhraseAI

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PhraseAI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered phrase generator application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or use the app, we may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifier (no email required for basic use)
  • Language preferences
  • Notification preferences (Premium users)

Content You Create

  • Generated phrases - stored locally on your device
  • Published phrases - when you share to the Discover Feed, these become public
  • Likes and saves - your interactions with community content

Usage Data

  • App feature usage (anonymized)
  • Generation count (for free tier limits)
  • Crash reports

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized phrases using AI
  • Display community content in the Discover Feed
  • Determine "Phrase of the Day" based on community votes
  • Send notifications (Premium users who opt-in)
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Enforce usage limits for free tier

AI Processing

PhraseAI uses OpenAI's API to generate phrases. When you request a phrase:

  • Your selected tone and language are sent to OpenAI
  • No personal information is included in AI requests
  • Generated content is not used to train AI models

Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

Community Features

When you publish a phrase to the Discover Feed:

  • The phrase becomes publicly visible to all users
  • Your anonymous profile may be associated with the phrase
  • Other users can like and save your phrases
  • You can delete your published phrases at any time

Data Storage

  • Local data - Stored on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Cloud data - Community content stored on Supabase (secure cloud infrastructure)
  • iCloud sync - Premium users can sync across devices via Apple's iCloud

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI - AI phrase generation
  • Supabase - Backend and database
  • Apple Push Notifications - Notifications (Premium)
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management

Data Retention

  • Local phrases are retained until you delete them
  • Published phrases remain until you delete them or delete your account
  • Account data is deleted upon request

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your generated phrases at any time
  • Remove your published phrases from the community
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Disable notifications

Children's Privacy

PhraseAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating this page.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: info@wilderlopezelias.com
